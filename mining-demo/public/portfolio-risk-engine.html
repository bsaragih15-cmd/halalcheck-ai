Model & governance notes

A single-period, common-factor portfolio twin of MIND ID. Seven shared factors are sampled together around a macro latent (so China, the commodity cycle and FX co-move and diversification can fail across assets at once), mapped to subsidiary upstreaming through calibrated sensitivities, then rolled through holding obligations — including debt principal repayments — to a residual Holding-FCF distribution. PTBA, Antam, Inalum and Timah are quantified; Freeport is visible but excluded from quantified cash. Mitigation re-runs every path under the same random draws (common random numbers) so before/after deltas isolate the management action. All numbers are illustrative placeholders until validated against FY2025 actuals, ownership, dividend receipts and holding obligations — confidence tags travel with every output. Not investment advice.
