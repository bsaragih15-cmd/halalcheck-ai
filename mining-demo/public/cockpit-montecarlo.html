Model & calibration notes

A single-period driver twin of PT Bukit Asam (PTBA) within MIND ID, calibrated to PTBA's public order of magnitude (≈40 Mt production, ≈62% domestic via DMO, ~Rp 9 T EBITDA, ~Rp 38 T revenue, 75% payout, 65.9% MIND ID ownership). The four drivers are sampled together with a realistic correlation structure (coal↔demand positive, coal↔IDR negative, demand↔freight positive). Illustrative, not investment advice — the value is the shape of the risk and the breach probability, not pinpoint figures. This re-implements the cockpit's deterministic model in the browser; per the design brief, the durable version wires Monte Carlo into the cockpit's own DCLogic from source so the two share one source of truth.
