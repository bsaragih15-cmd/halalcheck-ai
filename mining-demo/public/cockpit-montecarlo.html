Model & calibration notes

A single-period driver twin of PT Bukit Asam (PTBA) within MIND ID, calibrated to PTBA's public order of magnitude (≈40 Mt production, ≈62% domestic via DMO, ~Rp 9 T EBITDA, ~Rp 38 T revenue, 75% payout, 65.9% MIND ID ownership). Drivers are sampled together with a realistic correlation structure (coal↔demand positive, coal↔IDR negative, demand↔freight positive). Mitigation re-runs every path under the same random draws (common random numbers), so the before/after comparison isolates the effect of the management action. Illustrative, not investment advice. Per the design brief, the durable version wires this into the cockpit's own DCLogic so the two share one source of truth.
